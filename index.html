He is a full professor at College of Computer Science, Chongqing University , Chongqing, China. He obtained Ph.D. degree from Pierre and Marie Curie University and Institut Mines-Télécom/Télécom SudParis, France in 2014. He received the B.Sc. and M.Sc. degrees in Control Science and Control Engineering from Northwestern Polytechnical University, Xi'an, China, in 2007 and 2010, respectively.

   His current research interests include: Intelligent Unmanned System, Human Mobility Behavior Modeling, Spatial and Temporal Trajectory Mining and Analysis, Taxi Trajectory Data Compression and Visualization, Trajectory Representation Learning and Intelligent Mobility. He has served as a guest editor of Journal of Ambient Intelligence and Humanized Computing (TODO), Applied Sciences (TODO), and so on. He has served as the chairman of 15th International Conference on Green, Pervasive and Cloud Computing (GPC'20), HHME 2020: International Conference on Human Health and Medical Engineering(HHME’20), International Conference on Ubiquitous Intelligence and Computing (UIC'19), and so on. He serves as an associate editor of Springer Journal of Big Data Analytics in Transportation(TODO), He serves as vice-chairman of the 13th China Conference on Internet of Things (Wireless Sensor Network) (TODO)

  He got published over 100 papers including 30 ACM/IEEE Transactions. His work on taxi trajectory data mining was featured by IEEE Spectrum in 2011 and 2016 respectively. He was the recipient of the Best Paper Runner-Up Award at MobiQuitous 2011.
